A Simple Bookmark

Manager

A clean and simple interface to organise your favourite websites. Open new browser and see your sites load instantly. Try is for free.

Features

Our aim is to make it quick and easy for you to access your favourite websites. Your bookmarks sync between your devices so you can access them on the go

  • Simple Bookmarking
  • Speedy Searching
  • Sharing

Bookmark in one Click

Organise your bookmarks however you like. Our simple drag-and-drop interface gives you control of how you manage your favourite sites.

Intelligent Search

Our Powerful search feature will help you find saved sites in no time at all. No need to trawl through all your bookmarks

Share Your bookmarks

Easily share your bookmarks and collections with others. Create a shareable link that you can send at the click of a button.
